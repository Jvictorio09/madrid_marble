What we do

Comprehensive marble fabrication & installation UAE wide—with precision tooling, on-site teams, and dedicated project managers for villas, hotels, and commercial towers.

Marble Installation

Precision setting for villas, hotels, and corporate lobbies with meticulous leveling, mitred edges, and joint finishing. On-site teams deliver fast UAE installation schedules.

Custom Fabrication

CNC profiling, waterjet cutting, bookmatching, and bespoke inlays crafted in our Dubai facility, ensuring flawless execution for architectural marble works.

Restoration & Polishing

Renew worn surfaces with crystallization, sealing, and scratch removal, preserving the brilliance of luxury marble installation Dubai wide.

Architectural Consultation

Material sourcing, sampling, and technical submittals for architects and designers, ensuring optimal stone selection for every application.

Maintenance Contracts

Scheduled inspection and upkeep programs for hotels, resorts, and commercial towers to maintain impeccable stone surfaces year-round.

Answering your questions

Still exploring marble suppliers in Dubai or Abu Dhabi? Our team is ready to guide you with samples, site visits, and detailed quotations.

Get in touch
Do you provide free quotations?

Yes. Share your drawings or scope and we will provide a detailed quotation with material options within 24 hours.

What’s the average lead time for installation?

Standard villa or apartment fit-outs take 3–5 weeks from approval. Larger commercial projects are scheduled with phased delivery plans.

Do you import marble or stock locally?

We maintain ready stock in our Dubai warehouse and source globally from Italy, Turkey, and Greece for specialty finishes.

Can I visit your Dubai showroom?

Absolutely. Book a showroom visit to review slabs, finishes, and consult with our material specialists.

Do you handle cutting & polishing on-site?

Our mobile teams handle on-site adjustments, polishing, and sealing to ensure a flawless installation.

Do you provide warranty on installation?

Yes, all installations include a workmanship warranty along with maintenance guidance tailored to your project.

Can you work directly with architects or designers?

We collaborate closely with architectural and design teams, providing samples, technical submittals, and value engineering.
