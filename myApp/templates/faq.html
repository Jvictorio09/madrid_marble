Most projects take between 3–10 working days once measurements are confirmed and materials are ready. Larger villa or commercial projects may require phased timelines, which we outline clearly before we start.

Durability & Maintenance

Marble is a natural material — with the right care, it ages beautifully. Here’s what we usually recommend.

How do I maintain marble?

We provide basic after-care guidelines after every project. Typically, we recommend gentle pH-neutral cleaners, prompt wipe-up of spills, and periodic sealing for certain stone types and finishes. Our team can also advise on suitable sealants and maintenance routines based on how the space is used.

Daily

Soft cloth + pH-neutral cleaner for general wipe-down.

Spills

Wipe quickly, especially for acidic liquids like coffee or juice.

Periodic

Sealing as recommended for the stone and finish used.

Warranty & Peace of Mind

Every installation comes with a workmanship guarantee that covers installation-related issues within a defined period. We want you to feel confident that if something doesn't look or perform as it should, our team will step in to review and resolve it.

Exact coverage details — including duration and inclusions — are confirmed in your quotation and project agreement.

In simple terms

If there is an issue linked to how we cut, fix, or finish the stone, we treat it as our responsibility to investigate and propose a solution — not something you have to chase.

Natural variations in stone (veining, colour shifts, micro-pitting) are part of marble’s character and are not considered defects — but we do our best to align expectations before fabrication starts.
